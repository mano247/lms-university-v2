Steps to Enroll

Information Session: We recommend familiarizing yourself with our faculties and study programs. You can find details here.

Application: To apply, complete the application form available on our website. The application includes basic personal information, your educational qualifications, and your study program preferences.

Registration: Before applying, make sure to register here. Registration allows you to track your application status and receive all relevant updates by email.

Documentation: Attach required documents such as transcripts, diplomas, and references. Review the list of required documents to ensure you have submitted everything needed.

Interview: After reviewing applications, candidates who meet the criteria will be invited to an interview. The interview is an opportunity to meet members of our admissions committee and further present your qualifications.

Enrollment Decision: After interviews are completed, all candidates will be notified of results and the enrollment decision. We will provide guidance on next steps and information about the start of classes.
