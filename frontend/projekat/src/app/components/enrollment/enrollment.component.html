Enrollment Steps

Information Session: We recommend familiarizing yourself with our faculties and study programs. You can find details here.

Application: To apply, fill out the application form available on our website. The application includes basic information about you, your educational qualifications, and your study program preferences.

Registration: Before applying, make sure to register here. This registration will allow you to track the status of your application and receive all relevant information via email.

Documentation: Attach the required documents such as transcripts, diplomas, and recommendations. Review the list of required documents to ensure you have submitted everything needed.

Interview: After reviewing applications, candidates who meet the requirements will be invited to an interview. The interview is an opportunity to meet members of our admissions committee and further present your qualifications.

Enrollment Decision: After the interviews are completed, all candidates will be notified of the results and the enrollment decision. We will guide you through the next steps and information about the start of classes.
